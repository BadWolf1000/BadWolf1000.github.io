Capture the Flag

// how it works

Getting Started

01

Join the Discord

Everything runs through our Discord. Join at discord.gg/Snah2S3Wv2, introduce yourself, and react in #roles to pick your interests.

02

Come to a Meeting

Weekly meetings are open to everyone. We cover tools, walkthroughs, and competition prep. Meeting times and locations are pinned in Discord.

03

Pick Your Path

Interested in offense? Join the Red Team. Defense? Blue Team. Competition puzzles? CTF squad. Or just come to learn — all paths are valid.

04

Start Competing

Once you're comfortable, jump into a CTF or Red vs Blue exercise. The team competes in NCL each semester — spots are earned through participation.

From complete beginners to experienced hackers — we have something for everyone. Beginners learn from veterans, veterans sharpen skills by teaching.

// common questions

FAQ

Do I need experience to join? ▼
No experience required at all. We welcome complete beginners — the only requirement is curiosity and a willingness to learn.
Do I need to be a CS or IT major? ▼
Nope. Cybersecurity pulls from many fields — critical thinking, problem solving, and persistence matter more than your major.
Is there a membership fee? ▼
No fees to join the club or Discord. Some competitions like NCL have individual registration costs, but club membership is free.
How do I get on the competition team? ▼
Show up to meetings, practice with the team, and participate in club events. Competition spots are given to active members — the more involved you are, the better your chances.
What time/day are meetings? ▼
Meeting times are posted in Discord each week. Join the server and check #announcements for the latest schedule.
Can I join mid-semester? ▼
Yes — jump in whenever. The Discord is always active and meetings are open all semester long.

Ready to get started?

Join the Discord and say hi — that's all it takes.
